Jump Around, and MAKE Your Way

Play Techno-Jump, the all new scrolling platformer from GamersIRL.

User Guide

Press the Green Flag to begin.


Click 'Play', then choose a number to try out a premade level. We recommend you start with level 1 and work your way up.

Or you can click 'Create', then 'Create New' to make your own levels.


Playing

Default movement keys are:

LEFT - left arrow

RIGHT - right arrow

JUMP - up arrow

You can change these in the main menu settings.

Press the grey arrow in the top left to reset.

Press the purple arrow in the top right to exit.


Inventory

OPEN INVENTORY - down arrow

You can change this in the main menu settings.

While in the inventory, click on a block you want to place. Then, double click to place it at your mouse position. You cannot place blocks that are faded.


Creation

Press the gear icon in the top left to open the in-game settings. In here, you can:

Change music tracks.

Change background music.

Save the levels you have created.

Press the eraser icon in the top left to erase a block. Click and hold on the block you wish to erase.


Blocks

FLOPPY DISC - Increases jump height

CD - Changes your size when you touch it

CABLE - You can walk across it if you are not small

VIRUS - Moves side to side and kills you if you touch it

FIREWALL - Kills both you and viruses if they touch it

PASSWORD - Grants a one-time immunity to firewalls


But, most importantly, have fun!
